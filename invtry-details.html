ERP Modules

  • Dashboard: View key metrics and reports.
  • Customer: Manage customer information and relations.
  • Supplier: Manage suppliers and their details.
  • Category: Organize products or services into categories.
  • Stock: Manage stock levels and inventory.
  • Sales: Track and manage sales transactions.
  • Expense: Record and manage business expenses.
  • Buy: Handle purchase orders and acquisitions.
  • Staff: Manage staff and employee information.
  • Reports: Generate and view various reports.
  • Settings: Configure system settings and preferences.
